Hey my name is Rob Machado, stoked you're here and that we're able to share the stoke. Ever since coming off tour I have found pleasure in the simple things like riding fun boards with great people in beautiful places. Here at "Rob Machado Surfboards," you will find an array of my favorite shapes. Most of my boards are made from epoxy foam and glassed with e-polly resin. With the help from my sponsor, Firewire I have been able to continue to be a professional surfer and now a shaper / designer.
